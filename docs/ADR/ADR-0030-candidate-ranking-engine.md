## Context

Mission 29 added multi-symbol funding scanning.

The scanner finds and rejects/approves individual symbols, but DeltaGrid needs a second-level ranking layer that aggregates scanner output with stability, liquidation risk, execution-cost risk, and rejection reasons.

This mission still does not execute trades.

---

## Decision

Add candidate ranking engine.

## Ranking Logic

The ranking engine aggregates:

- scanner verdict
- annualized funding
- net expected edge
- edge-to-cost ratio
- liquidity score
- funding score
- basis risk penalty
- scanner score
- walk-forward verdict
- liquidation risk verdict
- execution cost verdict
- rejection reasons
- composite score
- final GO / NO-GO verdict
