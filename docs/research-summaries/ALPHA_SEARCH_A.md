# Alpha Search A

> **Explanatory summary**

## What this record covers

Alpha Search A was the macro-risk-regime research family. Its preserved records
cover the feasibility check and the decision to stop before building a strategy.

## Question investigated

The proposed research asked whether lagged macro risk conditions could improve
low-turnover BTC spot long-or-cash outcomes after realistic costs. Before that
question could be tested, the protocol required causal evidence showing when
each input would first have been available to a decision-maker.

## Evidence and controls

The required first-availability lineage could not be established to the frozen
standard for the SP500 series. Provider or series substitution, timing changes,
sample-threshold reductions, parameter rescue, and protocol redesign after the
failure were not authorized.

The access record shows no BTC archive or BTC price/return-field access, no
strategy signals or P&L evaluation, and no validation or holdout performance
access. No strategy, backtest, or dry-run was created for this search.

## Result

The status `REJECTED_BEFORE_STRATEGY_BUILD` means the search stopped at data
feasibility. It does not mean a strategy was backtested and found unprofitable;
strategy construction and result-bearing testing never occurred. The record
makes no conclusion about whether macro regimes can predict BTC.

## Why it matters

The rejection protected research integrity. Proceeding without trustworthy
causal availability would have allowed look-ahead uncertainty into every later
result. Stopping was therefore a valid research outcome, not an engineering
failure.

## What this does not authorize

These records do not authorize acquiring replacement data, changing the frozen
requirements, resuming the search, or moving to validation, holdout, paper,
live, capital, ML, or autonomous stages. The [final freeze](../DELTAGRID_FINAL_FREEZE.md)
controls current authorization.

Exact source records control precise values and historical wording. Any
discrepancy must be resolved in favor of the source record, not this summary.
