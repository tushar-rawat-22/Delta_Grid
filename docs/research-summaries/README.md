# DeltaGrid research and evidence summaries

> **Explanatory summary collection**
>
> These pages explain preserved DeltaGrid research and evidence for human
> readers. They do not replace the linked source records, change their results,
> reopen research, or authorize paper trading, live trading, capital deployment,
> ML, or autonomous execution.

## Purpose

This directory provides plain-English companions to immutable research records
and deterministic machine evidence. It helps reviewers understand the question,
controls, evidence, result, and present boundary without rewriting the source.

The exact source records control precise values and historical wording. The
[final freeze](../DELTAGRID_FINAL_FREEZE.md) controls current authorization. If
a summary differs from a source record, resolve the discrepancy in favor of the
source record.

1. [Final freeze](FINAL_FREEZE.md) — the completed platform, negative research

## Raw evidence and human explanation

Contracts, evidence JSON, checksum manifests, and preserved research documents
are the source records. These summaries explain their relationships and negative
outcomes; they are navigation, not a new research contract or authorization
instrument.
