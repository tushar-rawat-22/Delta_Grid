# Project audit trail

> **Explanatory summary**
>
> This page explains preserved DeltaGrid research and evidence for human
> readers. It does not replace the linked source records, change their results,
> reopen research, or authorize paper trading, live trading, capital deployment,
> ML, or autonomous execution.

## What this record covers

The changelog records the repository's chronological evolution: components,
tests, research phases, outcomes, and safety statements as they were recorded.
The decision log records important choices and the reasons given at the time.
Together they are preserved audit trails, not a current plan.

## Question investigated

These records answer two historical questions: what changed as DeltaGrid grew,
and why were important engineering, research, and safety choices made?

## Evidence and controls

Entries connect work to files, verification, decisions, and, where recorded,
commits. Repeated safety boundaries show how the project separated research and
simulated infrastructure from real orders and capital. Later entries document
the shift from open-ended platform building to finite falsification.

A reviewer should use the changelog to establish sequence and the decision log
to understand rationale. An entry labelled active, approved, next, or GO belongs
to its historical phase unless a later controlling record says otherwise.

## Result

The audit trail shows substantial infrastructure development and repeated
strategy rejection. Later research decisions and the final freeze supersede
earlier next actions. The final research outcome remains that no validated alpha
or selected candidate exists.

## Why it matters

Preserving both records makes changes and reversals visible. It prevents an old
roadmap, paper-only approval, or proposed next mission from being mistaken for
current authority, while retaining the reasoning needed by future reviewers.

## What this does not authorize

Historical implementation, test, readiness, paper, AI, or autonomy language
does not reopen research or authorize paper trading, live trading, capital, ML,
or autonomous execution. Current authorization is controlled by the
[final freeze](../DELTAGRID_FINAL_FREEZE.md), not by an earlier log entry.

Exact source records control precise values and historical wording. Any
discrepancy must be resolved in favor of the source record, not this summary.
The final freeze controls current authorization.
