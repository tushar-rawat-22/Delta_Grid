# DeltaGrid final freeze

> **Explanatory summary**
>
> This page explains preserved DeltaGrid research and evidence for human
> readers. It does not replace the linked source records, change their results,
> reopen research, or authorize paper trading, live trading, capital deployment,
> ML, or autonomous execution.

## What this record covers

The final-freeze records consolidate the completed platform, the negative
research outcomes, the absence of a selected candidate, and the controls that
keep later operational stages closed.

## Question investigated

After the authorized research families were exhausted, the project had to
decide whether any evidence justified continued alpha search or progression to
implementation, paper, live, capital, ML, or autonomous stages.

## Evidence and controls

The platform completed deterministic research contracts, data provenance and
certification, causal features, simulation, cost and risk controls,
chronological evaluation, statistical falsification, tracked evidence,
checksums, and regression tests. Those capabilities establish a research
platform; they do not establish an economic edge.

The freeze preserves Alpha Search A's feasibility stop, Alpha Search B's four-
candidate development rejection, the earlier family rejections, zero selected
candidate, and sealed Alpha Search B validation and holdout performance. The
verification record and checksum manifest support the freeze publication by
checking identities, scope, tests, and protected files.

The immutable verification evidence records the freeze-era full suite as 731
passed with one third-party warning. Later documentation-suite totals belong to
later repository changes and are not part of that immutable freeze-era count.

## Result

The machine status `COMPLETED_RESEARCH_PLATFORM_NO_VALIDATED_ALPHA` means
DeltaGrid is a completed quantitative research platform with no validated
profitable strategy. No candidate was selected, all authorized alpha families
were exhausted, and alpha discovery stopped. Completed infrastructure and
passing tests did not change that negative economic conclusion.

## Why it matters

The freeze prevents repository size, dashboards, models, scores, reviews, or
test success from substituting for validated alpha. It preserves useful
engineering and negative evidence while stopping result-guided expansion after
the predeclared research budget was exhausted.

A future versioned reopening contract would have to govern genuinely new
information and freeze the rationale, causal data, assets, venue, time splits,
candidates, experiment budget, costs, statistics, validation, one sealed
holdout, parity, paper-only boundary, expiry, and separate capital and live
gates before result-bearing work begins.

## What this does not authorize

The freeze authorizes no new strategy construction, validation or holdout
access, Freqtrade or FreqAI translation, paper trading, dry-run, live trading,
orders, capital deployment, ML training, model promotion, or autonomous
execution. No dashboard, model, test suite, score, or review can independently
change those boundaries.

The current [final-freeze explanation](../DELTAGRID_FINAL_FREEZE.md) controls
authorization. Exact source records control precise values and historical
wording. Any discrepancy must be resolved in favor of the source record, not
this summary. The final freeze controls current authorization.
